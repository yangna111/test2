Volunteer in De Anza Oaks!

Volunteering in our community allows it to thrive. There are many opportunities to lend your talents within De Anza Oaks. Please consider being an active part of our wonderful community.
  • DAO Website Co-Administrator

    Description:

    Help to manage the DAO website to keep it up to date and maximize timely communication within our community.

    Contact:

    Alicia Schober or John Krischer - Co-Administrators.
    Landscape Committee

    Description:

    This committee works closely with our Community Manager and our Landscape contractor in all aspects of landscape management. Decisions are made on plants, trees, irrigation and fire clearance among others.

    When:

    Meets monthly

    Where:

    Rotates among members homes

    Contact:

    Bill Milam or Lani Harriman
  • DAO Block Leaders

    Description:

    De Anza Oaks residents who wish to improve communication and increase public safety in the neighborhood are encouraged to sign up for Block Leader training through the City of Cupertino. In De Anza Oaks, we would like to see one Block Leader for every 30 households. These Leaders would serve as an important liaison to the Board and community for matters from social events to emergency response. To register for Block Leader training, e-mail www.communityrelations@cupertino.org or call 408-777-3331.

    Contact:

    Alicia Schober or John Krischer - Co-Administrators.
